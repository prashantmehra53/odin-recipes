Lasagna

  • 1 1/2 lb. ground beef
  • 1 lb. hot breakfast sausage
  • 2 cans (14.5 ounce) whole tomatoes
  • 3 c.lowfat cottage cheese
  • 1 package (10 ounce) lasagna noodles
  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix cottage cheese, beaten eggs, grated Parmesan, 2 more tablespoons parsley, and 1 more teaspoon salt. Stir together well. Set aside. Add 1/2 teaspoon salt and the olive oil to the boiling water, then cook the lasagna noodles until “al dente” (not overly cooked). Drain.
  3. To assemble: Arrange 4 cooked lasagna noodles in the bottom of a baking pan, overlapping if necessary. Spoon half the cottage cheese mixture over the noodles. Spread evenly. Cover cottage cheese with a layer of mozzarella cheese. Spoon a little less than half the meat/sauce mixture over the top. Repeat, ending with meat/sauce mixture. Sprinkle top generously with extra Parmesan.
